Find the section titled "Static files".
Click "Add a new mapping".
In the "URL" field, enter your MEDIA_URL (the same value you have in your settings.py, usually /media/).
In the "Directory" field, enter the absolute path to your MEDIA_ROOT directory on the PythonAnywhere server (the same value you have in your settings.py, e.g., /home/your_username/your_project/media/).
